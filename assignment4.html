HCDE533 Assignment 4

Background

The goals of this assignment is to design a box that is made up of flat panels with fingerjoints/boxjoints, tabs, or another joining method design for sheet material. Panels and all tabs need to be 3d printed, but cannot 3D print the box in one piece. Design for an interference fit - when we press the panels together they should join tightly and not need additional fasteners and screws. Furthermore, they should provide a close enough fit that your resulting box can hold something like sugar without leaking.
